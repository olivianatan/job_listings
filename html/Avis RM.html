House, United States Avis Budget Group Full time

Job Title: Spanish Speaking-Revenue Management Analyst
Job Type: Full Time Permanent
Location: Bracknell Head Office Based (Hybrid Working)
Salary: £30,000 plus benefits and holiday
  • Are you a graduate with a business, science or economics degree?
  • Do you have excellent analytical skills?
  • Do you speak and write Spanish?
  • Are you looking for a great opportunity to progress your career in a fast-paced, commercial environment where you will be recognized for your valued contribution to company performance?

Then you should join the Revenue Management team at Avis Budget Group
Revenue Management is a centralised department that supports and advises on pricing, inventory management and on pricing strategy for our renowned Avis and Budget car rental brands. Our Revenue Management Analysts work with data from a variety of internal and external sources to advise on pricing and yield strategy.
This critical activity requires consistent communication with other departments, data gathering and analysis as well as managing tools and processes to enable high quality revenue management.
This is an excellent opportunity for a graduate with excellent analytical and communication skills to play a key role in a busy team which is central to the company’s profitability. We offer an energetic, collaborative working environment and an open culture that will give you the opportunity to rapidly take on increasing responsibility while gaining recognition, career progression and the opportunity to work with senior colleagues.
Key responsibilities for this position:
  • Managing optimization process, focusing on distribution, pricing, segment management and demand management
  • Demand forecast management
  • Recommendation of pricing strategy and pricing tactics
  • Supporting the development and maintenance revenue management process and tools
  • Delivering high quality quantitative analysis to support own recommendations
  • Communication and relationships with key stakeholders in other departments
  • Support communication and reporting for regional board

Job requirements:
  • Educated to degree level, ideally in a business related or science subject
  • Excellent level of Spanish Speaking
  • An internship or any kind of professional experience in analytical roles would be highly regarded
  • Excellent analytical ability, attention to detail, and the ability to work both independently and as part of a team
  • Ability to analyse complex scenarios and solve problems, formulate recommendations and communicate them effectively to senior management
  • Customer focus: understanding the needs of internal and external customers and responding promptly
  • Communication skills: balancing talking and listening in meetings, communicating ideas simply and clearly, confident representing own views, regularly sharing information and ideas
  • Completing work to a high level of accuracy and detail, maintaining focus, energy and effort when under pressure
  • Planning and delivering; working hard to achieve or exceed targets and objectives, plans and prioritising work effectively
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office applications

Avis Benefits:
  • 33 days holiday inclusive of bank holidays
  • Employee Assistance Programme (EAP) provided by Health Assured – 24/7 support for your mental health and wellbeing
  • Employee discounts on car rental across Avis Budget Group (including Zipcar).
  • Pension contribution
  • Friends and family discount
  • Bracknell HQ has free parking and access to a small onsite gym

Avis Budget Group is a leading global provider of mobility solutions, operating three of the most recognized brands in the industry through Avis, Budget and Zipcar, the world’s leading car-sharing network. Together our brands offer a range of options, from car and truck rental to on-demand car sharing that bring vehicles closer to where and when customers need them, by the minute, the hour, one-way, daily, weekly and months-long reservations. Our brands represent a mobility ecosystem of more than 11,000 locations in approximately 180 countries. With more than 70 years of experience in managing global fleets and with one of the largest fleets of connected cars in the world, we are committed to innovation and are moving the future of mobility forward.
