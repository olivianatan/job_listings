      JOB PURPOSE

      MSC Cruises is a market leading cruise provider in the Mediterranean, South Africa and South America, sailing year-round in the Mediterranean and the Caribbean.

      Perfect for recent graduates in the Finance/Business/Economics field, this role is responsible for developing UK & Ireland pricing architecture proposals & setup accordingly, in Cruise only and Fly & Cruise formula.


      KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES

      - Manages set-up of Fly & Cruise and Cruise Only, strategic and tactical pricing

      - Reviews cruise by cruise performance throughout the selling phase, recommending solutions on pricing and air travel commitments.

      - Liaises with HQ Revenue Management for regular pricing reviews on assigned areas of business

      - Prepares setup forms and monitor loading through the whole value chain and for the whole process, until it is loaded in the system

      - Tests and confirm correct functioning and loading of prices onto our booking engines

      - Looks after bonus commission management including loading, checking, communicating and documenting

      - Gives indication to the Marketing Team about the product to feature on weekly email communications to direct consumers and travel agencies

      - Supports the UK Contact Centre for issues related to pricing & product

      - Prioritises, together with the Marketing Team visibility of our products on communication materials/channels and regularly check visibility of products on our systems, particularly during the New Campaigns/Promo setup period

      - Performs regular product and price benchmarks for the area of business and has full awareness of what is offered by competitors


      QUALIFICATIONS

      - Fluent in spoken and written English

      - Educated to degree level is preferable, professional qualifications will also be considered

      - Excellent IT skills, with good command of MS Office suite and strong data manipulation abilities through Excel (Pivot, V Look Up, Index, Match functions)

      - Ability to operate autonomously and establish key priorities for self and others

      - Ability to be decisive and when required to challenge the status quo, instigating and managing change effectively

      - Ability to maintain a positive and approachable attitude while working under pressure

      - Availability to travel when required, mainly within the EU.


      VISA REQUIREMENTS

      - Full Right to Work in the UK required
