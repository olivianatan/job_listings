Senior Strategic Pricing Analyst
Santa Clarita, CA or Remote

From its modest beginnings in 1965 with a single ship cruising to Mexico, Princess has grown to become one of the premiere cruise lines in the world. Today, its fleet carriesits passengers to more worldwide destinations than any other major line.

While cruising paused worldwide, we laid the groundwork to take our company to even greater success, with fresh perspectives and new ways of working. We’re looking fora Senior Strategic Pricing Analystto help lead our efforts.

The Senior Strategic Pricing Analystwill work with teams across Princess, with our office Santa Clarita, CA, we’re looking for amazing humans across the country for this role in our Market Planning Department. Princess offers strong compensation and outstanding benefits including cruise travel.

Here’s a summary of what Princess is looking for in itsSenior Strategic Pricing Analyst.Is this you?

Responsibilities

  • Develops strategic cruise pricing for senior management review by analyzing booking trends associated with previous pricing actions.

  • Prepares ROI analysis, statistical reports; analyzes and interprets data; and develops ad hoc reports through a variety of data warehouse applications.

  • Responsible for providing program release and redeployment information to facilitate loading in the reservation system.

  • Researches and prepares data on competitor pricing, programs and policies.

  • Manages communications with Marketing and reviews Marketing material for accuracy.

  • Responds to pricing and policy requests from other departments.

  • Performs miscellaneous assignments, as required.

Requirements

  • Completion of a Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience.

  • Minimum of four years of related experience in analysis, financial or revenue management.

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

  • Ability to meet deadlines, maintain a high level of attention to detail and accuracy, and carry a task forward from beginning to end with minimal assistance, while working in a team or group environment.

  • Proficient in Word and PowerPoint.

  • Advanced knowledge of Excel with a working knowledge of complex spreadsheets and database applications.

  • Experience with Tableau and SQL a plus.

Benefits

  • Cruise and Travel Privileges for you and your family

  • Health Benefits

  • 401(k)

  • Employee Stock Purchase Plan

  • Training& Professional Development

  • Rewards & Incentives
