• Description
• There’s never been a more exciting time to join United Airlines.
We’re on a path towards becoming the best airline in the history of aviation.
Our shared purpose – Connecting People, Uniting the World – is about more than getting people from one place to another.
It also means that as a global company that operates in hundreds of locations around the world with millions of customers and tens of thousands of employees, we have a unique responsibility to uplift and provide opportunities in the places where we work, live and fly, and we can only do that with a truly diverse and inclusive workforce.
And we’re growing – in the years ahead, we’ll hire tens of thousands of people across every area of the airline.
Our careers include a competitive benefits package aimed at keeping you happy, healthy and well-traveled.
From employee-run "Business Resource Group" communities to world-class benefits like parental leave, 401k and privileges like space available travel, United is truly a one-of-a-kind place to work.
Are you ready to travel the world?If you’ve ever wondered how an airline figures out the price of their tickets, our Pricing & Revenue Management team can tell you.
By predicting consumer demand and keeping up to date on market trends, the Pricing & Revenue Management team sets the pricing structure for all our routes, both international and domestic.
Get your tickets ready – for an exciting new career journey that will help us run a successful airline.Key Responsibilities:
Associate Analysts / Analysts are placed into either Pricing or Revenue Management:
• Pricing
• – Defines price points based off customer’s willingness to pay and the competition
• Revenue Management
• – Forecasts customer demand and sets overbooking levels+ Analyze trends in bookings, revenue and market dynamics+ Assess demand patterns and competitive landscape+ Segment customers based on market conditions+ Develop fare structure/demand forecast to optimize revenue+ Plan seasonal strategy for holidays and peak travel periods+ Communicate department strategy, working with other collaboratorsFirst Year Expectations:
• Onboarded as part of a class of new hires+ Paired with peer mentor throughout training+ 6+ weeks of training with department leaders; graduate to market management+ Shadow PRM peers in cross functional teams (RM vs Pricing) and meet Commercial division peers (Network Planning, Sales, Marketing)+ Department DEI Mentorship programs and listening sessions offered+ Participate in community service and social eventsYour Career:
• Lateral and promotional opportunities to experience different fields and market teams+ Serve as a mentor to new analysts and build leadership experience+ Cross functional project work to drive growthPlease note:
This position may be hired at the Associate Analyst level or the Analyst level, depending on candidate background, skill level and experience.
• Qualifications
• United values diverse experiences, perspectives, and we encourage everyone who meets the minimum qualifications to apply.
While having the “desired” qualifications make for a stronger candidate, we encourage applicants who may not feel they check ALL of those boxes! We are always looking for individuals who will bring something new to the table!What’s needed to succeed (Minimum Qualifications):
• Bachelor's Degree+ 1-3 years of equivalent experience+ Track record of outstanding analytical and problem solving skills+ Strong verbal and written communication skills and the ability to work well with others+ Ability to apply quantitative skills and business sense to airline industry+ Must be legally authorized to work in the United States for any employer without sponsorship+ Successful completion of interview required to meet job qualification+ Reliable, punctual attendance is an essential function of the positionWhat will help you propel from the pack (Preferred Qualifications):
