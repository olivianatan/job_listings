Title:  Senior Analyst Route Planning

Location: 

Long Island City, NY, US, 11101

Req ID:  52013

 

 

____________________________________________________________________________________

Position Title: Senior Analyst Route Planning
____________________________________________________________________________________

Position Summary:

JetBlue is looking for a Senior Analyst Route Planning to be based at JetBlue’s Support Center in Long Island City, NY. The Route Planning team is responsible for determining JetBlue’s growth strategy and maximizing the profitability of JetBlue’s route network. The team’s shorter term responsibilities include actively determining new routes, optimizing service levels of existing routes, and performing ad-hoc network and competitive analyses. 

 

Longer term responsibilities include working cross-functionally across the company to advise on major fleet and strategic growth decisions.  The ideal candidate has a passion for aviation, a deep understanding of airline route networks and strategy, strong problem-solving abilities, and developed quantitative and inter-personal skills. At JetBlue we take our mission to Inspire Humanity very seriously. As such, we are looking for someone who embodies our five values of Safety, Caring, Integrity, Passion, and Fun.

Essential Responsibilities:

  • Contribute to the development of JetBlue’s route network strategy and pursue its implementation through capacity planning
  • Extensively research potential new markets, prepare profitability forecasts, and build cases for market entry
  • Analyze JetBlue Airways performance on a route by route basis and identify areas of opportunity; monitor industry capacity changes
  • Serve as the primary owner of 1-2 Route Planning business processes
  • Serve as a primary point of contact in Route Planning for internal and external partners
  • Participate in mentoring and training new Crewmembers
  • Perform ad hoc fleet- and network-related projects in support of senior leadership
  • Build relationships with airport partners across the route network
  • Support other teams including the Airline Planning, Commercial, Finance, Government Affairs, Corporate Communications, and Investor Relations divisions
  • Other duties as assigned

Minimum Experience and Qualifications:

  • Bachelors’ degree in aviation, business, economics, mathematics, finance, public policy or a related field
  • Three (3) years of work experience in aviation, finance, management consulting, or a related field
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint
  • Strong written and verbal communication ability
  • Must pass a pre-employment drug test
  • Must be legally eligible to work in the country in which the position is located

Preferred Experience and Qualifications:

  • One (1) year of work experience at an airline in Network Planning, Revenue Management, or Finance
  • Experience with database querying (e.g., SQL), automation languages (e.g., Python, R), and data visualization (e.g., Tableau)

Crewmember Expectations:

  • Regular attendance and punctuality
  • Potential need to work flexible hours and be available to respond on short-notice
  • Able to maintain a professional appearance
  • When working or traveling on JetBlue flights, and if time permits, all capable Crewmembers are asked to assist with light cleaning of the aircraft
  • Must be an appropriate organizational fit for the JetBlue culture—that is, exhibit the JetBlue values of Safety, Caring, Integrity, Passion and Fun
  • Promote JetBlue’s #1 value of safety as a Safety Ambassador, supporting JetBlue’s Safety Management System (SMS) components, Safety Policy and behavioral standards
  • Identify safety concerns, issues, incidents or hazards that should be reported and report them whenever possible and by any means necessary including JetBlue’s confidential reporting systems (Aviation Safety Action Program (ASAP) or Safety Action Report(SAR))

Equipment:

  • Computer and other office equipment

Work Environment:

  • Normal office environment

Physical Effort:

  • Generally not required, or up to 10 pounds occasionally, 0 pounds frequently. (Sedentary)

Compensation: 

  • The base pay range for this position is between $64,000.00 and $103,100.00 per year. Base pay is one component of JetBlue’s total compensation package, which may also include access to healthcare benefits, a 401(k) plan and company match, crewmember stock purchase plan, short-term and long-term disability coverage, basic life insurance, free space available travel on JetBlue, and more.
